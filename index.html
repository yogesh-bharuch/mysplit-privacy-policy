Privacy Policy – MySplit

MySplit (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address (via Firebase Authentication)

User Data

  • Expense details
  • Group information
  • Payment records
  • Uploaded receipts/invoices (stored in Firebase Storage)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Enable expense sharing within groups
  • Store and display expense and payment data
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data is only shared:

  • With members of the same group to enable expense tracking
  • With Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Storage) for backend functionality

Data is shared only as required for app functionality and is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Account Deletion & Data Removal

Users have the right to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion:

  • Send an email to yogesh_bharuch@yahoo.co.in
  • Use the subject: "Delete MySplit Account"
  • Include your registered email address in the request

What data will be deleted:

  • User account information (name, email)
  • All group memberships
  • Expenses and payment records
  • Uploaded receipts/invoices

Data retention:

  • All user data stored in Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage) is permanently deleted within 7 days of receiving the deletion request
  • No personal data is retained after deletion

If you have any questions, contact us at yogesh_bharuch@yahoo.co.in

6. Children’s Privacy

MySplit does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app or this page.
